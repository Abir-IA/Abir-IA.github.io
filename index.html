Abir Alaoui Ismaili

Current position

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Université de Lorraine, in the MPSI Departement of the CRAN laboratory.

My research focuses on Digital Twin ecosystems as intelligent, data-driven frameworks for monitoring, decision-making, and optimization. The reliability of these systems depends on accurate, secure, and well-governed data, ensuring consistency and trust in real-time operations. To make Digital Twins more adaptive and responsive, I integrate process mining to uncover inefficiencies and anomalies and ensure alignment with real-world processes, while Multi-Agent Systems (MAS) enable them to have more adaptability in changing environments. My work bridges theoretical and applied perspectives, particularly in interdisciplinary domains such as healthcare and smart manufacturing. I am currently working on JUNEAU project, a project funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR) [ANR-22-CE46-0010-01, running from October 2022 to October 2026], which aims to develop a Digital Twin for the CHU Saint-Etienne Emergency Department. The objective is to enable real-time visualization, predictive capabilities, and decision support to optimize patient flow and reduce emergency waiting times.
